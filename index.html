ABOUT

Carol is a So-Cal native, yoga teacher, scientist, and music/travel/dog lover truly finding balance and gratitude living in DTLA. First exploring yoga while healing a back related injury, she gravitated towards sharing yoga as an organic development of her dedicated personal practice.

She received her 200 hour certification from Jay Co in 2013 and continues studies in alignment-based Power/Vinyasa Flow, Restorative yoga, and Meditation, all in support of mindfulness in movement and stillness.

Her modern yogi experiences reassures a welcoming path to inspire ones own true potential to discover the inherent wellbeing that resides within us all.
